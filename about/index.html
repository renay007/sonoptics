About Us

Founded in November 2014 and based at the City College of New York, Sonoptics Systems is developing devices that monitor, evaluate, and inform people about the current state of their pipe. Whether you are a homeowner, a building owner, or a contractor, our devices combined with a well-designed app will allow you to instantly know your pipe's status and to take actions within seconds. And with the different type of services we offer, Sonoptics Systems is the easiest way for people to get in touch with our recommended list of technicians and plumbers to fix an issue in a timely manner.

At Sonoptics Systems, we believe that everyone deserve peace of mind and should be in the comfort of their home watching cat videos. Let us worry about your pipes and help you save money by preventing costly damages due to pipe failure.

Our Mission

At Sonoptics Systems we are developing technologies for measurements and monitoring. Our team combines its knowledge in emerging laser technologies and non-linear optics, with well-established electronic and acoustic systems. We are constantly looking for alternatives to an easy and affordable monitoring technology. In doing so, we provide assurance and quality, while facilitating reliability and material studies.

Our Co-Founders

Rene Midouin

CTO & Co-Founder

Rene is the co-founder and CTO at Sonoptics Systems. He oversees the technical strategy of the company, and is dedicated to building a team of world-class engineers to keep Sonoptics Systems at the forefront of the industry. Rene draws on his experience as a software developer at MTA, as well as his development of Android and iPhone games. He will earn in 2016 a bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from the City College of New York with a minor in Mathematics.

Paul Merisier

CEO & Co-Founder

Paul is the co-founder and CEO at Sonoptics Systems. He drives the company's vision, strategy and growth as it provides interesting and unique ways for potential customers to optimize buildings or facilities diagnostics. Paul is currently the Vice Chair of the Education Society's Chapters Committee and also a 2014 nominee of the IEEE MGA Leadership Award. Paul draws on his experience from his participation at multiple research projects, and as a former R&D Intern at Corning Inc. Paul will earn his Masters in Electrical Engineering with a Photonics emphasis from the Grove School of Engineering in May, 2015.

Richard Sam

CPO & Co-Founder

Richard is the co-founder and CPO at Sonoptics Sytems. He oversees the product conception, development, and strategy in order to provide a comprehensive service and solution. He builds on his experience as a Design Engineer during his internship at The City College of New York. Richard also met co-founders Paul Merisier and Rene Midouin at The City College of New York where he earned a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering.
