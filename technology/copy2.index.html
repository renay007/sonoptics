About us

With the growing awareness of the current state of pipe infrastructure in New York, our startup is developing a system that monitors pipe integrity for the detection of cracks and corrosion.

Our Co-Founders

Rene Midouin

CTO & Co-Founder

Rene is the co-founder and CTO at Sonoptics Systems. He oversees the technical strategy of the company, and is dedicated to building a team of world-class engineers to keep Sonoptics Systems at the forefront of the industry. Rene draws on his experience as a software developer at MTA, as well as his development of iPhone games. He will earn in 2016 a bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from the City College of New York with a minor in Mathematics.

Paul Merisier

CEO & Co-Founder

Paul is the co-founder and CEO at Sonoptics Systems. He drives the company's vision, strategy and growth as it provides interesting and unique ways for potential customers to optimize buildings or facilities diagnostics. Paul is currently the Vice Chair of the Education Society's Chapters Committee and also a 2014 nominee of the IEEE MGA Leadership Award. Paul draws on his experience from his participation at multiple research projects, and as a former R&D Intern at Corning Inc. Paul will earn a Master in Electrical Engineering with a Photonics emphasis from the Grove School of Engineering in May, 2015.

Richard Sam

CPO & Co-Founder

Richard is the co-founder and CPO at Sonoptics Sytems. He oversees the product conception, development, and strategy in order to provide a comprehensive service and solution. He builds on his experience as a Design Engineer during his internship at The City College of New York. Richard also met co-founders Paul Merisier and Rene Midouin at The City College of New York where he earned a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering.
